About Us

VISION


To see AAG-Dasma the hub in producing theologically matured pastors creating outreach churches both in rural and urban places with the Christian message of love and hope and to lead people into life-changing encounter with the Lord Jesus Christ.

MISSION


To expand the ministry of AAG to every town and city through evangelism, discipleship, and church planting.

CHURCH MOTTO


“Be Holy” (1 Peter 1:15-16)

AFFIRMATION OF FAITH


• The Holy Bible as the anointed words of God inspired by the Holy Spirit.
• One God eternally existing in three divine persons: God the Father, God the Son, God the Holy Spirit.
• Jesus Christ is the only begotten Son of God, conceived of the Holy Spirit and born of a virgin.
• The fall of man.
• The divine plan of salvation through grace and redemption.
• New birth and water baptism by immersion.
• The holiness of life.
• The holy communion.
• The baptism of the Holy Spirit.
• The power of divine healing.
• The second coming of Jesus Christ.
• Eternal life for the righteous and eternal punishment for the wicked.
